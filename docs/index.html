Flatty Documentation & Installation Guide
In order to install and setup the Flatty Theme properly you should take a look into this guide and follow all mentioned steps as well as instructions as good as possible. If you have problems or questions nevertheless, don't hesitate to ask for help by creating a thread at the MyBB support forums. Feel free to recommend / star the theme in case you like it. :)

Getting Started

Before you can even actually start using and configure the Flatty Theme you have to upload all files inside the Upload folder.

1. Once that's done head to your Admin Control Panel   ›   Templates & Styles and click on Import a Theme.
2. Click the Choose File button and search for the flatty-theme.xml file which is located right below the upload folder.
3. Give the theme you've just imported a name of your choice, check Ignore Version Compatibility and then click on Import Theme.

Header Image

You could either upload an image with the same name & dimensions to avoid changing anything here or upload your own image. Keep in mind that you have to adjust the css if you are going to use your an image with a different name and custom dimensions. The image should be atleast 200px in height. Here's how to change the name or dimensions:

1. First head to your Admin Control Panel  ›  Templates & Styles  ›  Flatty  ›  global.css
2. And then click on Edit Stylesheet: Advanced Mode.
3. Scroll down to line 56 or search for #logo and change the url/name or dimensions of your image.

Logo Image

You can either link to your own or an external image or simply upload an image with the same name. The logo should be atleast 50px in height (assuming you didn't change the height of the header).

1. Ok, first log into your Admin Control Panel.
2. Head to Templates & Styles   ›   Flatty
3. Scroll down to the bottom and find Board Logo.



4. Change / Adjust the path to your image (can also be an external image).
5. Scroll down even further and then press Save Theme Properties. - Done!

Sidebar + it's content

Here is how you can customize the Custom Sidebox on the index page:

1. First log into your Admin Control Panel.
2. Now head to Templates & Styles  ›  Templates  ›  Flatty Templates  ›  Index Page Templates  ›  index
3. Scroll down and find line 19 and 26.
4. Edit the placeholder text on the mentioned lines and then click on Save and Continue Editing or Save and Return to Listing.



And here is how you can remove the Custom Sidebox:

1. Do the exact same thing as above (only the first 2 steps though).
2. Scroll down and find line 16 and 30.
3. Delete everything inside those lines or everything inside the table including the table itself. (See screenshot)

Who Is Online

By default the Who Is Online content should look different on your end. That's because you have to edit it manually unfortunately, atleast if you want to have it look like on the screenshot.

1. First log into your Admin Control Panel.
2. Head to Configuration  ›  Languages  ›  Your Language  ›  Options  ›  (Edit Language Variables)  ›  index.lang.php
3. Scroll all the way down to the bottom and find online_note

About Us

Here I'm going to show you how can customize the About Us text.

1. First log into your Admin Control Panel.
2. Now head to Templates & Styles  ›  Templates  ›  Flatty Templates  ›  Footer Templates  ›  footer
3. Scroll down and find line 28 - 30.



4. Delete / Customize the plain placeholder text inside the <h2></h2> and <span></span> tags.
5. Click on Save and Continue Editing or Save and Return to Listing - Done!

Footer Links

Here is how you can customize the Footer 'Item' Text:

1. First log into your Admin Control Panel.
2. Now head to Templates & Styles  ›  Templates  ›  Flatty Templates  ›  Footer Templates  ›  footer
3. Scroll down and find line 44, 46, 52 and 58.



4. Customize the placeholder text within the <span></span> tags of the mentioned lines above.
5. Click on Save and Continue Editing or Save and Return to Listing - Done!


And here is how you can customize the Social Media Links:

1. Do the exact same thing as above (only the first 2 steps though).
2. Scroll down and find line 32 - 38.



3. Edit / Remove the already existing links or add your own.
4. Click on Save and Continue Editing or Save and Return to Listing.

If you also want to customize the Footer Menu Links you basically have to do the exact same things explained above. Everything necessary is located in the Footer Templates.
